How much does it cost to have a book written? The real figures

by Giovanni Casali, editorial coordinator

Having a book professionally written costs from 2,500 euros for a short project, 6,000-15,000 for a full biography, more for a complex novel. The price depends on three things: how long it is, how much research it needs, and how many hands it must pass through before it is right.

What drives the price

Three factors, always the same. Length: a hundred-page family story and a four-hundred-page manual are not the same job. Research: a book born from interviews takes hours of conversation and transcription; one that needs archives, documents and verification takes weeks. And the passages between hands: drafting, revision, proofreading: every extra round is quality you pay for and can see. Publishing (cover, layout, stores) may be included or remain a separate chapter.

The options, honestly

A professional studio costs more and delivers a finished work, with a contract, confidentiality and a chain of revision. A single freelancer can cost less: it all depends on who you find, and you discover the difference halfway through. Doing it yourself with AI costs nothing and it shows: everyone recognises generic text, no one recognises your voice in it. A book that has to last deserves hands that know what they are doing.

What to watch out for

Rock-bottom prices: writing well takes time, and whoever gives it away is saving money on your book. Books promised in two weeks. Anyone who will not put a contract in writing, with deliveries, payments and confidentiality. The serious trade is recognised by how it treats these three things.

Quick questions

Can I pay in instalments?

You pay by stages of progress: you always know what you are paying for and what you are receiving, from the first chapter to delivery.

Does the price include publishing?

Writing and publishing are two separate chapters: the publishing package (cover, layout, EPUB, stores) starts at 1,500 euros and is added only if you want it.

I have already written something: can you fix just that?

Yes: it is called editing or rewriting, and it costs less than writing from scratch. Bring us the text and we will tell you what it needs.
