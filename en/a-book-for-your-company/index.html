by Giovanni Casali, editorial coordinator

Your company's history, or the book that positions you as the reference in your field. Written by professionals, signed by you: the business card no competitor can copy.

Why a business book

A book opens doors a brochure does not. It is given to important clients and does not end up in the bin with the gadgets. It establishes you at conferences before you even speak. And it lasts: in ten years the social campaign will be forgotten, the book will still be on the desk of the people who matter.

The two possible books

The first is the company's history: for an anniversary, a generational handover, a family that wants to record how it all began. The second is the positioning book: your expertise turned into authority, the classic founder's book that makes an entrepreneur the reference point of his field. Often, halfway through, you discover the right book is a little of both.

How we work with an entrepreneur

We know your time is the scarce resource: the method is built around that. A few hours of interviews, arranged around your diary, remotely if needed; the rest (research, archives, writing, revisions) is ours. Total confidentiality on figures and strategy: contract and NDA before we start, as with every project of ours.
