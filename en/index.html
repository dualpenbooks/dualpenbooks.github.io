The studio

DualPen Books is an independent editorial studio: we write, edit and publish books, our own and our clients'.

Our collections are the proof of the craft: 40 technical manuals, a series published in 14 languages, a historical saga in ten novels, 290 editions in the catalogue, produced end to end, from text to cover, from layout to store distribution.

For our clients we do exactly the same: we take your story, your expertise or your idea and turn it into a finished book, well written, published where it matters, in the language that matters.

We are a small studio with a full crew's toolkit: writing and rewriting, historical and documentary research, interviews and transcriptions, professional double-reviewed translation in four languages, cover design, print and EPUB layout, store publishing and print on demand. All under one roof, with no handovers.

How we work

  1. Free first consultation, by video call: you tell us about the project, we tell you frankly whether and how it can be done, and what it costs.
  2. Contract and total confidentiality: NDA, the book is yours, the rights are yours, our name appears nowhere.
  3. Working sessions by video call, recorded only with your consent, transcribed and turned into chapters you read and approve step by step.
  4. Payment by milestones: you always know what you are paying for and what you receive.
  5. Delivery in the format you want: a manuscript ready for a publisher, or a finished book on sale in the stores.
