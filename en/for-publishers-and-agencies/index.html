DualPen Books also works for the trade: ghostwriting for your authors, series editing, double-revision translation in four languages, layout and full production. Your imprint on the cover, our work inside. No one will know: that is the trade.

What we do for a publisher

Third-party ghostwriting: your author's, personality's or client's book, written by us and signed by them. Series editing and revision, when the in-house desk is stretched or missing. Editorial translation into Italian, English, French and German, with a double-revision chain. Print and EPUB layout. And the turnkey series: from editorial plan to the stores, all under one roof.

Proof of craft

We do not ask for blind trust: our in-house production counts 50 titles, 290 editions in the catalogue and a series published in 14 languages, produced end to end, from text to cover to distribution. And the writing can be read: our excerpts, five genres, five samples.
