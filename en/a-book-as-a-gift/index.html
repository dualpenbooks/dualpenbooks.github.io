A book as a gift: the idea no one forgets

by Giovanni Casali, editorial coordinator

For a milestone birthday, a wedding anniversary, a retirement: the story of a loved one, gathered by voice and turned into a real printed book, to place in the grandchildren's hands. The gift that does not end up in a drawer.

Why a book

Because yet another object is forgotten within a month, and the story of a life is not. A book with their name on the cover is the gift that is read, reread, passed on to children and grandchildren, and every time it tells the person who receives it: your story is worth keeping. No shop window sells that.

Two ways to give it

The first is to give the journey: the person receives the gift and then tells their story, meeting after meeting, to the one who writes it. The hours spent telling it are already part of the present, and often the most beautiful part. The second is the surprise: we gather the voices of family and friends, the photographs, the memories, and the book arrives wrapped, ready to open. Two different roads to the same result: a life that becomes a book.

Occasions and timing

An eightieth birthday, a golden wedding, a retirement, a special Christmas. A real book takes months, not weeks: the interviews, the writing, the chapters to approve, the printing. The rule is simple: start early. For Christmas, start in the summer; for a fixed date, let's talk as soon as possible and build the calendar backwards.

What it costs

A full biography starts at 6,000 euros; a shorter project, the story of one period or a single tale, from 2,500. The exact quote comes after the first consultation, free and without obligation. The full picture: How much does a family biography cost?

Quick questions

How early should I start?

Months, not weeks. For Christmas, start in the summer; for a spring birthday, start in winter. If the date is close, ask us anyway: sometimes a shorter format arrives in time.

What if they do not like talking about themselves?

We start from the voices of those who love them: children, grandchildren, lifelong friends. What emerges is a choral portrait, and often the person, reading it, adds by themselves what they would never have told.

Can I print just a few copies, only for the family?

Yes. With print on demand the book can exist in five copies or five hundred, with no warehouse: as many as your family needs.
