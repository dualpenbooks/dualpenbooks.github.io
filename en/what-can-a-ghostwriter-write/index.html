What can a ghostwriter write? Not just biographies

by Giovanni Casali, editorial coordinator

Everything made of words: manuals, essays, novels, business books, children's books, poetry, speeches. The biography is only the most famous genre of the trade. The rest can be explained with a single sentence, which is also the way we work: the expertise is yours, the craft is ours.

A ghostwriter is not a know-it-all

Better to say it at once: nobody writes well about what they do not understand, and anyone promising any subject lightly should be regarded with suspicion. A serious book on a serious subject is born in one way only: your expertise, our questions, research done properly. Before writing, one studies: the sources are read, those who know are interviewed, the facts are checked one by one. Then the text passes from hand to hand, from drafting to revision to proofreading, until it holds. It is workshop labour, with more eyes and more passes: the name on the cover remains one, yours.

Technical, scientific and professional manuals

The genre we know best: our own imprints count some forty technical manuals, with one series published in 14 languages. Procedures, regulations, popular science, the manual that gathers thirty years of your trade: you bring the knowledge, we turn it into a clear, orderly text that can be consulted without effort. It is the book that works for you: it introduces you, accredits you, makes you findable.

Essays, history and thought

Ancient and modern philosophy, spirituality and religions, historical research: these are the books where writing is half the work, and the other half is documenting well. Primary sources where they exist, an orderly bibliography, the courage to write "we do not know" where we do not know. If you have a thesis to defend or a tradition to tell, the essay is built together, with the rigour the subject demands.

Novels and historical fiction

You have the plot and the characters in your head, but not the time or the pen. The commissioned novel has always existed, and historical fiction is the house speciality: a ten-volume saga, written across three centuries of history, taught us how much period research it takes for a page set in the eighteenth century to ring true. It costs more than the other genres, and rightly so: the reasons are explained on the prices page.

Business books and specific fields

The company's story, the book that positions you in your industry, the technical text for your clients. Some fields we know from the inside, by direct experience and not only by trade: agriculture and the farming business, professional cooking, real estate, personal finance. In the others, the usual method applies: your expertise, our questions, the research.

And then everything else

Children's books, collections of poems to put in order and publish, speeches for an important occasion, short texts that must carry weight. Not all of them become months-long projects: some are weeks of work. We assess it together, in the usual place: the first consultation, free of charge.

Quick questions

My subject is very technical: how can you write about it?

We do not pretend to know it: we learn it from you. That is what the interviews are for, and the text returns to your eyes at every chapter: a technical error does not survive two readings.

Do you also write about religion or philosophy?

Yes, with the respect and documentation these subjects demand, and without putting our ideas into your book: the voice and the position are yours.
